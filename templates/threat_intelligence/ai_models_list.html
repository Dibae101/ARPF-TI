About AI Classifier Models

AI classifier models help automatically analyze and categorize threat intelligence data. These models can identify patterns, predict threat severity, and provide additional context to your data.

Supported Model Types

  • Scikit-learn pickled models
  • TensorFlow/Keras saved models
  • ONNX format models
  • Custom Python modules

Common Use Cases

  • Malware classification
  • Threat actor identification
  • Domain reputation scoring
  • Phishing URL detection
